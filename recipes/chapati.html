Chapati

Quick Whole Qheat Chapati Recipe

Chapati is indian wrap. This recipe is Whole Wheat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Water
  • 2 and half cups of Whole Wheat Flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon of Salt

Cooking Steps

  1. Form a dough by mixing the flour, water, and salt together in a bowl.
  2. Take the dough and roll it over a lightly floured surface.
  3. Knead the dough.
  4. Divide the dough into 8 pieces and roll them into a ball shape.
  5. Flatten each dough ball thinky with a rolling pin.
  6. Over a medium-hot griddle, cook each piece for about 2 mins.
  7. Flip over and cook the other side until both sides are lightly browned.

Nutriton Facts

  • 1g Fat
  • 27g Carbs
  • 5g Protein
